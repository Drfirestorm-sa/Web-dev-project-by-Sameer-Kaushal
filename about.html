Who I Am

Profile

Hi, I’m Sameer Kaushal — a BSc (Hons) Computer Science (Data Science & AI) student and the founder of Fravara.

I'm passionate about building intelligent, human-centred technology focused on accessibility, learning, and everyday productivity. My background in AI and cybersecurity drives me to create systems that are innovative, secure, and practical. During my university studies, I have focused on key modules such as Data Structures & Algorithms, AI Systems, Networking, and Entrepreneurship, which have shaped my technical and business mindset.

I founded Fravara as an AI-powered smart-glasses concept to help people interact with information naturally in real time. My design approach focuses on User-Centric Innovation, ensuring that complex technology is translated into intuitive experiences. Alongside my studies, I've gained industry exposure through placements at Precisely and Oriana Power, developing hands-on experience in technical support, automation workflows, and professional communication.
